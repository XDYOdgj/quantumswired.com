Embe 2-Way Baby Swaddle

A safe, breathable, and versatile swaddle for newborns 0-3 months. Designed to support healthy hip development while offering comfort and ease during sleep and feeding.

Soft and Breathable Fabric

Crafted from 100% natural Jersey cotton, this swaddle is gentle on your baby's delicate skin. Its breathable design helps regulate body temperature, ensuring a cool and comfortable sleep environment.

Customizable Swaddling Options

With the patented zip mechanism, you can easily adjust the swaddle to swaddle legs in for warmth or legs out for cooling. This flexibility helps prevent overheating and promotes better sleep for your baby.

Hip Healthy Support

This swaddle is approved by the International Hip Dysplasia Institute for its design that supports healthy hip development and joint mobility. It's an ideal choice for parents prioritizing their baby's long-term well-being.

Easy Diaper Changes

The Hook & Loop fastening system allows for quick and convenient access to the diaper area. You can easily unswaddle your baby without fully removing the wrap, making nighttime care more efficient and less stressful.

Safe and Secure Design

Featuring a no rise neckline, this swaddle ensures your baby sleeps safely without the risk of the blanket covering the face. It also provides a snug fit that helps your little one feel secure and relaxed throughout the night.

Why Choose Embe 2-Way Swaddle?

Whether you're looking for a safe and comfortable swaddle for your newborn or a convenient solution for nighttime care, the Embe 2-Way Swaddle is the perfect choice. It combines flexibility, practicality, and safety in one easy-to-use design.
