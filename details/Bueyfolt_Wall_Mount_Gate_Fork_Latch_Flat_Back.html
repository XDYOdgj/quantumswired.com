Bueyfolt Wall Mount Gate Fork Latch (Flat Back)

The Bueyfolt Wall Mount Gate Fork Latch is a sturdy and dependable choice for securely closing and locking your gate. Its flat back design blends seamlessly with walls or wooden gate posts, giving your outdoor space a clean, professional finish. Engineered for 1-3/8" X 1-3/8" gate frames, this latch ensures a universal fit for a wide range of gate hardware.

Easily install with the included screws, making it ideal for homeowners or businesses looking to upgrade or replace their existing gate hardware.
