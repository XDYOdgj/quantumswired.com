POSEAGLE 5-Inch Black Two Way Lockable Gate Latch S16100700

The POSEAGLE 5-inch black gate latch is a robust and dependable solution for securing livestock on your farm or ranch. Its two-way lockable feature allows you to open the gate from either side, ensuring seamless access without compromising on safety. Paired with a 4-digit combination lock, this latch provides an extra layer of security with 10,000 unique code options, making keyless entry both practical and worry-free.

Key Features
  • Two-way lockable design for secure gate access
  • 4-digit combination lock with 10,000 unique code options
  • Constructed for metal gates with tube diameters from 1-1/4" to 1-1/2"
  • Self-closing slam latch for convenient and safe gate operation
  • Sturdy solid steel build for long-lasting durability

Made from solid steel, this gate latch withstands harsh conditions and is built to outlast daily wear and tear. Its self-closing mechanism ensures your gate remains securely shut, reducing the risk of animals escaping and making herding more efficient. For maximum security, you can also attach a padlock, providing added reassurance for your property and livestock.

Whether you're managing a farm, ranch, or stable, this gate latch is designed to offer a balance of safety and convenience. Its reliable two-way functionality and durable metal construction make it an essential tool for anyone looking to enhance livestock containment while keeping gate operations simple and secure.
