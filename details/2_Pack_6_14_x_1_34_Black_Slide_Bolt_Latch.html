Lock Your Space with Reliable Slide Bolt Gate Latches

These 2-pack black slide bolt gate latches are made from high-quality 304 stainless steel, ensuring long-lasting durability and excellent corrosion resistance. Perfect for securing a variety of entry points, such as barn gates, shed doors, or even bathroom entrances, they combine reliability with ease of use. The robust 2-inch sliding bolt provides a secure lock without making it inconvenient to open or close.

Designed for versatility, these latches fit seamlessly on doors, windows, cabinets, and fences. With options to install vertically or horizontally in a flush mount style, they offer a simple and efficient solution without the need for complex tools or technical expertise. Each unit also features a built-in padlock hole, which elevates security and gives you peace of mind.

  • Forged from 304 stainless steel for unmatched strength and longevity.

  • Quick and simple installation without the need for special tools or skills.
  • Includes a padlock hole for an extra layer of security and protection.

Whether you're a homeowner or managing a commercial property, these latches provide a secure and stylish way to protect your space. They are an ideal choice for any DIY project, ensuring functionality and reliability without compromising on aesthetics.
