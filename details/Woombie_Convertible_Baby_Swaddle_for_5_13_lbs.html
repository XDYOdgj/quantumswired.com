The Woombie Convertible BabySwaddle

A revolutionary baby swaddle designed to mimic the calming feeling of the womb, helping your little one feel safe and secure as they sleep. The soft, breathable fabric is made from 95% cotton and 5% spandex, ensuring comfort for sensitive skin and all-day wearability.

Key Features

  • 95% Cotton & 5% Spandex for breathability and comfort
  • Self-soothing design that promotes better sleep and calmness
  • Multi-dimensional stretch with zipper for secure and adjustable fit
  • Arms in/out option for flexibility and milestone readiness
  • Seamless interior and easy one-step swaddling process

Why Choose Woombie?

This adaptable swaddle is perfect for newborns up to 3 months and weighs 5-13 lbs. It supports back sleeping, which is crucial for reducing SIDS risk, and allows for natural movement during sleep to prevent startling. With a simple two-way zipper and adjustable snaps, changing and swaddling your baby becomes effortless. Plus, it can be easily transformed into a wearable blanket for added versatility and convenience.

Perfect for Every Stage

Whether you're swaddling for bedtime or using it as a wearable blanket during the day, the Woombie Convertible BabySwaddle provides a practical and comforting solution for parents. Its stretchy, snug design moves with your baby and offers the gentle touch they love, making it a must-have for your baby's first few months of life.
